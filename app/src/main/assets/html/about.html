Description

NoiseCapture App is an Android App research project for measuring environmental noise using a smartphone. The goal is to produce relevant noise indicators from audio recordings, including a geospatial representation.

Project

NoiseCapture App is a collaboration between two french research laboratories, the Environmental Acoustic Laboratory (Ifsttar) and the DECIDE team of the Lab-STICC (CNRS).

Funding

This application is a part of the ENERGIC-OD research project (European Network for Redistributing Geospatial Information to user Communities - Open Data) [ http://www.energic-od.eu/].

ENERGIC-OD is co-funded by the ICT Policy Support Programme (ICT PSP) as part of the Competitiveness and Innovation Framework Programme by the European Community, and by the French geographic portal GEOPAL of the Pays de la Loire region (http://www.geopal.org).

Privacy

NoiseCapture respects your private life:

  • You can control how the data are transferred to the server, with the Data transfer options in the Settings parameters;
  • When Data transfer is enable, only anonymous data are uploaded;
  • There is no audio recording: only noise indicators are computed and uploaded to the server;
  • NoiseCapture needs only basic permissions that are are required for measuring noise, localizing your measurement and transferring data.
