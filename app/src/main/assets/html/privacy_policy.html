NOISECAPTURE PRIVACY POLICY (short)

This text is the short version of the privacy policy that governs your use of the software application NoiseCapture App (or NoiseCapture). 

You can access to the full online version of the NoiseCapture privacy policy (requires an internet connection).

NoiseCapture is a mobile application dedicated to the evaluation of the noise environment. It allows to perform noise measurements and, thus, to give information on your noise exposure. In addition, you can contribute to the realization of collaborative noise maps by anonymously sharing your measurements with the Community. All collected data, which can be optionally sent to a remote server, are used for scientific researches, in order to propose representations of the noise environment and of the community noise exposition.

NoiseCapture:
  • do not need any registration;
  • do not collect any personal data;
  • do not record any audio (noise indicators are calculated on the fly);
  • do not proceed to any background processing;
  • needs only basic permissions.
User:
  • can choose (or not) to transfer anonymous data to the remote server;
  • can stop all collection of information, if you don't use the Application or by uninstalling the Application;
  • has the ability to study the source code of NoiseCapture;
  • has the ability to access to all data that are shared with community.
If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at contact@noise-planet.org
